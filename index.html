Revoria Combat Test

REVORIA COMBAT TEST

Revoria Combat Test is a temporary 8.6 war server created to test, refine and rebuild the feeling of oldschool Tibia PvP. Players will create their own characters, enter the battlefield quickly and help us improve movement, healing, rune balance, death systems and overall combat through real fights and community feedback.
