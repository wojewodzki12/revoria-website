CURRENT FOCUS

REVORIA COMBAT TEST

Revoria Combat Test is a temporary 8.6 war server created to learn, test and rebuild the feeling of oldschool Tibia PvP. Players will create their own characters, enter the battlefield quickly and help us improve movement, healing, rune balance, death systems and overall combat through real fights and community feedback.

DEVELOPMENT APPROACH

BUILD IN PUBLIC

We show progress early, test ideas openly and let the community see how Revoria evolves.

REAL COMBAT DATA

Balance decisions come from actual fights, not theorycrafting in silence.

FAST ITERATION

Combat, healing, spawns and rewards will be adjusted step by step based on feedback.

REVORIA TEAM

Revoria is a small independent oldschool Tibia project built by players and tested with players. Instead of pretending to be a large studio, we focus on transparent development, real combat feedback and a clear direction for future worlds.
